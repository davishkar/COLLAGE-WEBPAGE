WELCOME TO JPKM, VITA

Jeevan Prabodhini Educational Institution College is a private college located in Sangli, Maharashtra, India. The college is accredited by AICTE. In addition to strong teaching, Jeevan Prabodhini educational institutions are also playing a leading role in research and innovation. The entire focus of the college is on academic activities, as evidenced by its infrastructure, extracurricular activities, and national and international collaboration. Our organization is an effort to bring higher education to the doorsteps of all economically and financially disadvantaged students in Changlang District, who cannot go outside the state for higher education.
